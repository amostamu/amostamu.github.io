Mathematics of Deep Learning and LLM-Driven Mathematical Research

Welcome to my research repository. Here, I explore the mathematical structures underlying deep learning and investigate how LLM can be leveraged to advance mathematical research.

Overview

As a mahematician, I am fascinated by the intricate mathematical foundations of deep learning and the potential of AI to revolutionize mathematical discovery. My work focuses on two main areas:

  1. Uncovering and analyzing the mathematical structures that underpin deep learning algorithms.
  2. Exploring innovative ways to use AI, particularly large language models (LLMs), as tools for mathematical research and problem-solving.

Research Interests

  • Topological and geometric aspects of neural networks
  • AI-assisted theorem proving and conjecture generation
  • Mathematical reasoning capabilities of LLMs
  • AIMO
